What Issues Matter the Most to Which Americans

Below you can see which demographics find which topics to be the most important to them in 2021. Participants can reply whether a certain topic is important with a yes/no answer.

Disclaimer: To make the viewing of each graph easier for the user, The charts are scaled to the data's relative percentile. Meaning instead of showing the maximum range that being 100%, The chart zooms in according to the highest percentile.
